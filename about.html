Crush 40

A band consisting of 4 members, well renowned in the Sonic The Hedgehog community for creating and composing some of the most famous songs in the series.

Members

  • Johnny Gioeli (Vocals, Writing)
  • Jun Senoue (Guitars, Arrangement)
  • Takeshi Taneda (Bass)
  • Katsuji Kirita (Drums)

Cover

The album cover used as the inspiration for this website is from the 20th Anniversary Edition of the Sonic Adventure 2 Soundtrack (The game which this song was made for and made it's debut in)

About the song

"Live and Learn" was originally created for the game "Sonic Adventure 2", and is played during the final boss fight of the game. It's a very powerful song, even without the context of an epic final battle, and it's lyrics relate very heavily to the arcs of the main characters throughout the game. It's also an extremely popular song amongst the community, being used in several other games and forms of media.
